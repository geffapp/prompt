“Act as a senior social media strategist. Your task is to transform the content of this website article into a high-performing Instagram post optimized for views and shares.

1) Input  
I will give you:  
• The article URL or pasted text  
• My company mission: [Mission]  
• My company values: [Value]  
• Any specific Instagram rules or preferences: [Best Practice]

2) Your goals  
• Maximize reach, saves, and shares  
• Stay fully aligned with my mission and values  
• Use Instagram best practices for 2025

3) What to create  
• A strong hook line (first 1–2 lines) designed to stop scrolling  
• A short, clear main message based on the article  
• A suggested structure (e.g., carousel with slide-by-slide ideas, or reel script outline)  
• Visual direction: colors, style, imagery, or B-roll ideas  
• Suggested call to action (CTA) for engagement and traffic  
• 10–20 hashtag ideas (mix of broad and niche)  
• A score out of 5 for:  
  – Traffic potential  
  – Scientific reliability  
  – Trending potential  
• One sentence explaining each score”
